Google Search & visibility

Why did I get an email from Google Search Console?

Google Search Console detected that pages from Wonderlabs are now appearing in Google Search results. The email is just a notification that Google has started collecting search data (impressions, clicks, and errors) for your site.

How can I see my site in Google Search?

Go to Google and search for:

site:2026wonderlabs.github.io/Wonderlabs

This shows the pages from Wonderlabs that Google has indexed. Over time, your site may also appear for normal keyword searches related to your content.

How do I check my search performance?

Visit Google Search Console and sign in with the same Google account that received the email. In the Performance report, you can see impressions, clicks, top pages, and top queries for Wonderlabs.

What if I don’t see my site in search yet?

It can take time for Google to fully index and rank a new site. Make sure your pages are accessible (no major errors), and keep updating the site with useful content. Over time, visibility usually improves.

Technical & site management

Where is Wonderlabs hosted?

Wonderlabs is hosted on GitHub Pages under the repository 2026wonderlabs.github.io/Wonderlabs. Any changes pushed to the repository will update the live site after a short delay.

How do I edit this FAQ page?

Open faqs.html in your code editor or on GitHub, edit the questions and answers inside the <details> blocks, then commit and push your changes. Refresh the live site to see the updates.

Can I add more questions?
